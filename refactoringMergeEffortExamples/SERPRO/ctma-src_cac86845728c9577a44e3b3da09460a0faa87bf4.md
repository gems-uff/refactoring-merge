This is not Rename_Class: the two types have different APIs (different getter names and one of them returns a different scalar kind), so a pure rename would have left the call sites unchanged. The case is therefore Change_Parameter_Type, not Rename_Class.

That fabricated line is the `++` entry. The refactoring was committed in Parent 2 prior to the merge; the merge merely paid the cost of integrating it with Parent 1's stylistic conventions on the same statement.
