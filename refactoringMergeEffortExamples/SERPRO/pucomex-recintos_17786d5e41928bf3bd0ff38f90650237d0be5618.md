## Interpretation
The refactoring is a **Rename_Method**: `obter` → `obterSUCE` on the aeroporto/cia-aérea
lookup services, introduced in one parent (which also marked the old `obter` as
`@Deprecated`, pointing callers to `obterSUCE`). The other branch continued to invoke the
old `obter` name, and additionally used a different accessor style (`...Service.get().obter`
vs `...Service.obterSUCE`). The merge therefore could not take either side verbatim; it
emitted `++` lines that combine the surviving accessor form with the **renamed** method
(`...Service.get().obterSUCE(...)`) and retargeted `AeroportoService`'s own internal call
to `obterSUCE`, while removing the now-obsolete `@Deprecated` shim. Because the renamed
method and its deprecation already existed on one parent's side, the `++` lines are
conflict-resolution merge effort attributable to a parent refactoring, not a rename
introduced by the merge.
